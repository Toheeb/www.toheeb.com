On Recoding app.grey.co

Grey.co has a fast service and slow onboarding; login page takes like-forever to load.

As a user and dev, who wants to promote personalizable web design, I recoded the page. Same Design but better results:

  • More than 20 times faster on mobiles and more than 10 times faster on desktops.

  • All document issues fixed with better better structure.

  • Users can personalize with any of all 360 Color Hues

  • Users can personalize with any typeface installed on their device.

  • and even more personalizations!
